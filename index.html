Leqa Coffee     HOME





Leqa provides 100% Arabica. Our offerings are premium value-added coffee products. We believe that our customers deserve the best coffee product and we carefully source top quality coffee beans and process with our unique roasting profile to consistently provide a quality product.


Understanding the unique needs of service providers, Leqa has developed a service package. Our service package is very convenient to use and easy to store.








Leqa Coffee is the antithesis of factory roasting. We source the finest beans from across Ethiopia and these are then re-selected by our team of the worlds finest artisan hand roasters. We then prepare and roast them only for your order on our magnificent ceramic roasters inside our Addis Ababa roasting atelier.


And we roast with the greatest of care in the same manner that’s been perfected here in Ethiopia for millennia - by hand, watching, smelling and hearing every single bean roast. It’s a magical process that allows us to bring out the absolute finest qualities from each bean. The result: The finest coffee that you have ever sipped.
